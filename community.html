What is Community?

A community is more than just a group of people; it's an evolving system of connected individuals striving to remind each other of their humanity.

What Community Means to Me:

  • For me, community lies in shared curiosity and a genuine desire to explore new ideas.
  • I find myself most at home and in community, with individuals who are intellectually curious and enjoy learning, questioning, and engaging in thoughtful discussions.
  • The connections helps inspire me to continuously grow and improve my understanding of the world.
